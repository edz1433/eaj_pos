What is EA POS Pro?About & Overview

EA POS Pro is an all-in-one cloud-based Point of Sale system designed for Filipino businesses of all sizes. Whether you run a small sari-sari store, a busy restaurant, or a multi-branch retail chain — EA POS Pro adapts to your workflow.

Built with a modern real-time dashboard and smart analytics, it gives you full visibility of your business so you can make better decisions — every single day.

Core Benefits
⚡
Lightning-Fast Checkout

Barcode scanning, quick product search, and one-tap checkout for faster sales.

📦
Real-Time Stock Tracking

Know your inventory levels instantly. Get low-stock alerts before you run out.

📊
Instant Reports

Daily, weekly, and monthly reports generated automatically — no manual counting.

🤖
AI-Powered Dashboard

Smart KPIs, sales trends, and performance insights from your own business data.

Multi-Device Support
