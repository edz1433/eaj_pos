Section 02

System Overview

The EA POS System is a modern, web-based application installed on your local computer or server. It runs on your private network — meaning your data stays on your premises, transactions process instantly even without internet, and you maintain full control over your business information.

🤖 AI-Powered Business Intelligence

Unlike traditional POS systems that simply record transactions, the EA POS System uses your own historical data to surface intelligent insights. The built-in analytics dashboard automatically identifies your best-selling products, peak sales hours, slow-moving inventory, expense trends, and stock loss patterns — giving you the information you need to make confident decisions without needing a separate accountant or analyst.

Section 03

Key Features & Modules

The EA POS System is a full-featured platform covering every aspect of your daily operations.

🛒

Sales / Billing

Fast, intuitive cashier screen. Supports walk-in, dine-in, and takeout. Multiple payment methods — cash, GCash, card, and others. Auto receipt printing.

📦

Inventory Management

Real-time stock tracking per branch. Low stock alerts. Damage, loss, expiry, and theft recording. Automatic stock deduction on every sale.

🏷️

Product Management

Full product catalog with categories, variants, bundles, and made-to-order items. Product images, barcodes, and per-branch pricing.

📷

Barcode Scanning

Instant product lookup by scanning barcode. Works with any USB barcode scanner. Reduces checkout time significantly and eliminates keying errors.

👥

Customer Management

Record customer names on transactions. Track purchase history. Apply personalized discounts and promotions.

🚚

Supplier Management

Manage supplier contacts and purchase orders. Track deliveries and payment status. Full goods-received workflow with inventory auto-update.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Daily summary, sales report, inventory report, expense report, ingredient usage, and stock loss report. Export-ready with PDF support.

👤

User / Staff Management

Role-based access — Super Admin, Administrator, Manager, and Cashier. Per-user permission control. Full audit trail of all staff actions.

🤖

AI Dashboard & Insights

Live analytics dashboard with 10+ KPI cards, 8+ interactive charts, top product rankings, hourly sales trends, payment mix analysis, and stock health overview.

💰

Cash & Expense Management

Cash sessions with opening/closing balance. Cash counts with over/short detection. Petty cash fund management. Full expense tracking by category.

🏪

Multi-Branch Support

Manage multiple locations from one system. Per-branch inventory, pricing, and staff. Consolidated reports across all branches for owners.

🍽️

Restaurant / Table Ordering

Dining table management by section. Open table orders with real-time tracking. Takeout and dine-in modes. Receipt per table with customer name.

🎁

Promotions & Discounts

Create percentage or fixed-amount promos. Apply to all products, specific categories, or specific items. Promo codes with expiry and minimum purchase rules.

⚙️

System Settings

VAT configuration, currency, receipt customization, branch setup, business type profile, and feature flags per branch. Full white-label ready.

💻

Offline / Local Setup

Runs entirely on your PC. No monthly cloud subscription. No internet dependency. Your data never leaves your premises. Fast and reliable at all times.

Section 06

Implementation Plan

Our proven implementation process ensures your system is up and running smoothly with minimal disruption to your business. Most deployments are completed within 3–7 business days.

1

Site Assessment & Requirements Gathering

We visit your location to assess hardware, network setup, and business requirements. We document your product catalog, pricing, branch setup, and staff roles. Duration: 1–2 hours

2

Hardware Setup & Software Installation

We install and configure the POS system on your PC, connect hardware (printer, scanner, cash drawer), and set up local network access. Duration: Half day

3

System Configuration

We configure your business profile, branch settings, VAT, payment methods, receipt format, and user roles. We customize the system for your specific business type. Duration: 2–4 hours

4

Data Entry & Product Setup

We encode your products, categories, pricing, suppliers, and initial stock levels. For larger catalogs, we provide a template for you to complete in advance. Duration: 1–2 days

5

Staff Training

Full hands-on training for your cashiers, managers, and admin. We cover daily operations, inventory management, reports, and cash session handling. Duration: 1–3 days (per package)

6

Go-Live Support

We are present (on-site or remotely available) on your first live day to ensure everything runs smoothly and resolve any questions in real time. Duration: 1 business day
